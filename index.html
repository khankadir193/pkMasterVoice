HOW TO PLAY

  • Top 8 Talents have to register their id’s with SK employees to participate in this event.
  • PK Singing Battle will be held between the talents.
  • Talents need to come live before 15min of the PK Battle.
  • Talents with maximum beans received from ROUND 1 will proceed to ROUND 2.
  • First Talent to the left in the webpage schedule, will start the performance first and will perform for First 5 – 6 Mins, Then Next Talent will perform for 5 – 6 Min.
  • Will have 1 winner based on maximum beans received in ROUND 2 will be mentioned in the BANNER for 3 days.
  • Talents and User will be ranked on the leaderboard based on the beans received and spent respectively in each round.
  • Top Gifter/ user in Round 2 of the tournament will be mentioned in the BANNER for 3 days.
  • Top 5 Talents and users in Round 2 will be mentioned in the web page.
